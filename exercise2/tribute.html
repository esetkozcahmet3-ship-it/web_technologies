Albert Einstein

About

Albert Einstein (1879–1955) — theoretical physicist who developed the theory of relativity. He received the 1921 Nobel Prize in Physics and is considered one of the most influential scientists of the 20th century.

Major Events

  1. 1905 — Published the theory of special relativity
  2. 1921 — Won the Nobel Prize in Physics
  3. 1933 — Immigrated to the USA
  4. 1939 — Signed a letter that led to the Manhattan Project

Contributions

  • Theory of Relativity
  • Photoelectric Effect
  • E = mc² equation
